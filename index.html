Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines eggs, cheese, and your choice of vegetables or meat.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan over medium heat, add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the eggs and swirl to cover the pan.
  4. Cook gently until the bottom sets but top is slightly runny.
  5. Add fillings and fold the omelette in half.
  6. Serve hot with toast or salad.
